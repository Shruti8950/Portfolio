About Me

SHRUTI

I'm a diligent and enthusiastic student currently studying at NIT Kurukshetra, pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Information Technology. Throughout my academic journey, I have developed a strong foundation in various programming languages, including C/C++, Python, HTML, CSS.

Aside from technical expertise, I also recognize the importance of effective communication and adaptability, qualities that are highly valuable in the IT sector. My involvement as an active member of the Shiksha society, where I contribute to teaching underprivileged students without any cost, showcases my sense of social responsibility and willingness to give back to the community.

Furthermore, I'm an NCC Cadet, which has instilled in me essential qualities like discipline, time management, and confidence, traits that can positively impact both my personal and professional life. In addition to my academic pursuits, I nurture my interests in sports and drawing, indicating a well-rounded personality with diverse hobbies.
